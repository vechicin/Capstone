Featured Speakers

Herbie Hancock

Piano

American jazz pianist, keyboardist, bandleader, and composer, who helped redefine the role of a jazz rhythm section and was one of the primary architects of the post-bop sound.

Christian McBride

Bass

is an American jazz bassist, composer and arranger. He has appeared on more than 300 recordings as a sideman, and is a seven-time Grammy Award winner.
